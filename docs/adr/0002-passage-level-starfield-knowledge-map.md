# Passage-Level Starfield Knowledge Map

The blog will model the reader-facing Starfield Knowledge Map at the Passage level rather than the Article or Concept level. A Passage is a meaningful excerpt from one published Article, and reviewed Passage Relationships connect Passages across Articles whenever possible.

This choice keeps the starfield useful for exploration instead of making it a decorative article list. Article-level stars would be too coarse to explain why two pieces of writing relate, while concept-level stars would add another node type and increase review complexity before the first reader-facing experience exists.

Starfield content will be generated in the administrator-facing Starfield Management area, reviewed before publication, and exposed to Readers only through a Published Starfield version. The first version will not expose Reader GraphRAG question answering, but Passage Text, Passage Anchors, Relationship Types, and Relationship Rationales will be retained so the same reviewed graph can support future grounded GraphRAG answers.
